# Tutorial to Learn English: Vocabulary Focus

This tutorial is designed to help you learn English by focusing on vocabulary. Mastering basic and thematic words is one of the fastest ways to improve your comprehension and communication skills. Here you will find vocabulary lists, useful phrases, and exercises to reinforce your learning.

---

## Why Learn English Vocabulary First

Learning vocabulary from the beginning provides immediate benefits:

- It allows you to communicate basic ideas without mastering all grammar.
- It increases your understanding when reading or listening to English content.
- It makes memorization easier by grouping words by themes.
- It is ideal for studying daily in short sessions.

# 4. Exercises to Practice English Vocabulary

## Exercise 1: Translation
Translate the following words into English:

1. Ventana  
2. Madre  
3. Arroz  
4. Verde  
5. Nosotros  

## Exercise 2: Basic Sentences
Create simple sentences with these words:

- house  
- milk  
- sister  

## Exercise 3: Guess the Word
Identify the English word based on the clue:

1. It is a color and appears on trees.  
2. It is a drink consumed daily.  
3. It is part of your family.  

---

# 5. Tips to Learn English Vocabulary Effectively

- Study between 5 and 10 new words per day.  
- Use flashcards to memorize vocabulary.  
- Repeat words out loud to reinforce pronunciation.  
- Learn full sentences, not just isolated words.  
- Watch English content with subtitles.  

---

Learning English through vocabulary allows you to progress quickly, improve comprehension, and communicate from day one. Consistent practice is key to seeing lasting results.
