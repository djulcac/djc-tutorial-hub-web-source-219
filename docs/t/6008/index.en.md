# Which Companies Are in the Nasdaq 100?

## 1. What Is the Nasdaq 100?

The **Nasdaq 100** is a stock market index that includes the **100 largest non-financial companies** listed on the Nasdaq exchange, ranked by market capitalization.

It mainly includes companies from the following sectors:

- Technology  
- Consumer  
- Biotechnology  
- Telecommunications  
- Industry  

Its composition is reviewed and updated periodically.

---

## 2. Why Is This Index Important?

- It is one of the most representative indexes of **technological innovation**.  
- Its performance is often driven by some of the world's most influential companies.  
- Many investors use it as a benchmark for growth-focused investing.  
- It can be easily replicated through ETFs such as QQQ.
